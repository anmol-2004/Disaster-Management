Earthquakes

Here you can include detailed information about earthquakes. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do stay indoors during shaking.
  • Do use a sturdy table or desk as cover.
  • Do listen to emergency broadcasts for updates.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t run outside during shaking.
  • Don’t use elevators as they may become inoperative.
  • Don’t move injured individuals unless they are in immediate danger.

Hurricanes

Here you can include detailed information about hurricanes. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do stay updated on weather forecasts and evacuation notices.
  • Do follow evacuation orders promptly.
  • Do avoid walking or driving through floodwater.

Tornadoes

Here you can include detailed information about tornadoes. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do take shelter in a small, windowless interior room or basement.
  • Do listen to weather alerts and follow safety advice.
  • Do check for injuries and provide first aid if necessary.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t seek shelter under a bridge or overpass.
  • Don’t leave your shelter until the danger has passed.
  • Don’t use elevators if your building has been damaged.

Dos:

  • Do create a defensible space around your property.
  • Do follow evacuation orders promptly.
  • Do have an emergency kit ready with essentials.

Tsunamis

Here you can include detailed information about tsunamis. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do move to higher ground immediately.
  • Do listen to official warnings and follow evacuation orders.
  • Do avoid walking or driving through floodwater.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t wait to feel the wave; move to higher ground as soon as you hear the warning.
  • Don’t return to the area until officials declare it safe.
  • Don’t stay in low-lying areas near the coast.

Volcanic Eruptions

Here you can include detailed information about volcanic eruptions. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do follow evacuation orders and seek shelter indoors.
  • Do protect yourself from ash fall by covering your mouth and nose.
  • Do stay indoors and keep windows closed to prevent ash from entering your home.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t drive unless absolutely necessary as ash can cause poor visibility.
  • Don’t use contact lenses during ash fall.
  • Don’t return to the area until authorities declare it safe.

Extreme Heat

Here you can include detailed information about extreme heat. This could include guidelines, safety tips, and response actions.

Dos:

  • Do stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
  • Do stay indoors during peak heat hours.
  • Do use fans or air conditioning to stay cool.

Don'ts:

  • Don’t leave children or pets in hot vehicles.
  • Don’t overexert yourself during extreme heat.
  • Don’t ignore symptoms of heat-related illnesses.
